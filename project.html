MOON STATION
PROJECT

Artemis was the twin sister of Apollo and goddess of the Moon in Greek mythology. Now, she personifies our path to the Moon as the name of NASA's program to return astronauts to the lunar surface by 2024, including the first woman and the next man. When they land, our American astronauts will step foot where no human has ever been before: the Moon’s South Pole. Through the Artemislunar exploration program, NASA will use innovative new technologies and systems to explore more of the Moon than ever before. We will collaborate with our commercial and international partners to establish sustainable missions by 2028. In the words of NASA Administrator, Jim Bridenstine, “And then we will use what we learn on the Moon to take the next giant leap–sending astronauts to Mars.”
