Our Mission

We are living in a new paradigm of equality and collaboration. Which makes it perfect to revamp our space exploration endeavors. In light of this, NASA has announced a new program known as Artemis, to establish American leadership, demonstrate new technologies, and inspire a new generation to pursue careers in STEM by landing a man and female together on the Moon. The incentive behind this program is to go back to the moon to prepare for settlement in celestial bodies. This is because the Moon serves as an excellent testing ground for settlement, since its environment is unfamiliar to life.

In part of their inspiration iniative, NASA has established opportunities such as NCAS ( NASA Community College Aerospace Scholars ). NCAS is run by NASA's very own talented workforce and hosts an anuall on-sight program. That selects a group of talented Community College scholars to engage with scientist and compete in a contest.

We were very fortunate to receive this opportunity to compete and be mentored by leading researchers of today. But because of the outbreak of COVID-19, the onsite experience was postponed and was changed to a virtual format. Although it would have been a once in a lifetime experience to visit a NASA site, we are still grateful to compete and meet NASA scientists virtually.

During our time as a team, we have met and listened to many NASA experts and interns about various topics via Microsoft Teams. We also met every day at 8 PM EST to discuss details about our project. Each of these meetings was conducted with light-heartedness and professionalism, therefore making us a friendly and productive team.

This website serves as an explanation for our project. So please feel free to contact anyone of us if there are any questions. Enjoy!
